Pasta Olio

Description

Your basic Italian pasta made with very few ingredients. Delicious, filling and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Spaghetti
  • ¾ cup - Olive Oil
  • ½-1 tsp - Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • ½-1 tbsp - Salt (for pasta water)
  • 1 tsp Salt (for mixture)
  • 4-8 - Garlic Cloves, minced
  • ¼ cup - Pasta Cooking Water
  • 1 bushel - Italian Parsley, roughly chopped

    • Optional:

  • Add zest and juice of 1 full lemon (or 2 tbsp lemon juice) to add some lemony goodness.

Directions

  1. Start pasta. Add around ½-1 tbsp Salt to water for flavor. Cook to al dente.
  2. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and cook, stirring continuously until fragrant, making sure it doesn't brown.
  4. Remove from heat temporarily, add the salt & red pepper flakes and stir to infuse with the oil.
  5. Add the pasta water, parsley (and optional lemon). Bring to a boil.
  6. Toss with cooked pasta.

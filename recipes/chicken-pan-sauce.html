Chicken with Pan Sauce

Description

Pan seared chicken breast with a pan sauce made from simple ingredients.
Takes a bit to make but is well worth it.

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless chicken breast, cut in half long ways.
  • 12oz chicken stock
  • olive oil
  • ½ shallot
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • juice of ½ lemon
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 pinches of parsley

Directions

Chicken

  1. Pat dry chicken breasts and season with salt & pepper
  2. Put around 1 tbsp olive oil in pan
  3. Cook chicken until semi firm and bouncy to the touch

Pan Sauce

  1. Reoil the pan a bit.
  2. Cook shallots and brown a little bit then add garlic.
  3. Add chicken stock.
  4. Turn heat to medium and start to reduce down.
  5. Add any chicken juices left from chicken resting.

    6. Note: Bubbles will begin to get bigger as stock reduces

  6. Add salt & pepper to taste
  7. When sauce has reduces by half add Butter
  8. Sauce is done when liquid sticks to the back of a spoon and is noticeably thick
  9. Reduce heat to low and add lemon juice
  10. Add parsley

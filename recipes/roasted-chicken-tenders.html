Roasted Chicken Tenders with Peppers & Onions

Description

Chicken tenders gussied up with peppers and onions. Best served over rice.

Ingredients

  • 1lb chicken tenders
  • 1 red, yellow or orange bell pepper, seeded and thinly sliced
  • ½ medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped garlic
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped pickled jalapeno peppers
  • ½ tsp freshly grated lemon zest
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped fresh oregano, or 1 tsp dried
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Whisk lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, jalapenos, oil and salt in a 9-by-13-inch glass baking dish.
  3. Add tenders, bell pepper and onion; toss to coat.
  4. Spread the mixture out evenly; cover with foil.
  5. Bake until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the middle, 25 to 30 minutes.
